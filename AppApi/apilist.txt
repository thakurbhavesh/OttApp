# Show Home Banner 

Access http://localhost/ott_app/AppApi/show_content.php (returns active content).
Filter by banner: http://localhost/ott_app/AppApi/show_content.php?status=active&banner=1 (returns content IDs 4 and 5).
Filter by top shows: http://localhost/ott_app/AppApi/show_content.php?status=active&top_shows=1 (returns content ID 6).
